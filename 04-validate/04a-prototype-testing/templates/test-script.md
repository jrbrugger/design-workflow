- There are no right or wrong answers — I'm testing the design, not you
- Think out loud as you go — tell me what you're looking at, what you're thinking
- If something is confusing, that's exactly what I need to hear
- We'll be done in about 30 minutes
- [If recording]: Is it okay if I record this for my notes?"
