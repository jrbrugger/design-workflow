## Warm-up (2 min)
_Build rapport. Don't jump into questions._

- Thanks for joining. Quick intro — I'm [name], I'm a designer at [company].
- We're exploring [general topic] — no right or wrong answers, I'm just trying to understand your experience.
- This will take about 30 minutes. Is it okay if I record for my notes? Only our team will see it.

## Context (5 min)
_Understand their world before diving into specifics._

1. Can you tell me a bit about your role and what a typical day looks like?
2. How does [relevant activity] fit into your workflow?
3. What tools do you currently use for [relevant task]?

## Follow-up Probes
_Use these when you need to dig deeper:_
- "Can you walk me through the last time that happened?"
- "What made that frustrating / easy / confusing?"
- "What did you try before that?"
- "If you could change one thing about that, what would it be?"
- "Who else is involved when you do that?"

## Wrap-up (3 min)
- Is there anything about [topic] that I didn't ask about but should have?
- Would you be open to a follow-up if we have more questions?
- Thank you — your input is really valuable.
